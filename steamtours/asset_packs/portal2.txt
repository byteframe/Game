Here are all the materials from Portal 2 converted into the Source 2 format for use in your VR environments! Please see https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2094270469 for more information on these asset packs. Unlike other Valve titles, this game (and asset pack) did not rely on a complete set of hl2 assets to function and uses comparatively fewer of those assets. I would have included the models in one download but this was too big for the VPK builder and it runs out of memory and crashes.
